# Connect Call AI Website

> Public crawling guidance for humans and AI agents. Use canonical URLs from `getSiteOrigin()` via `NEXT_PUBLIC_SITE_URL`.

## Discovery Endpoints
- [Robots](/robots.txt): Crawl policy source of truth.
- [Sitemap](/sitemap.xml): Canonical URL inventory for public pages.
- [RSS Feed](/feed.xml): Blog updates stream.

## Priority Public Pages
- [Home](/): Main product landing page.
- [Blog](/blog): Blog index and discovery hub.
- [Industries](/industries): Industry solutions overview.
- [Contact Us](/contact-us): Primary conversion/contact page.
- [Price Estimator](/price-estimator): Pricing-related entry point.

## Dynamic Public Collections
- [Industry Pages](/industries/*): Generated from `industries` data.
- [Published Blog Posts](/blog/*): Generated from published blog content.

## Crawl Boundaries
- Allowed: `/`
- Disallowed: `/ccai-admin` and `/ccai-admin/` (matches `app/robots.ts`)
- Ignore operational endpoints as content: `/api/*`

## Canonicalization Notes
- Prefer URLs listed in `/sitemap.xml` when duplicates exist.
- Treat admin/auth flows and API responses as non-document content.
